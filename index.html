Event Ambassador Criteria

  • Can be from any year or department.
  • Must register themselves for the event before applying as an ambassador.
  • Should actively promote the event and encourage registrations among their peers.
  • Must bring a minimum of 10 confirmed registrations to qualify.
  • Ambassadors achieving this target will receive an Event Ambassador Certificate.
  • Those with the highest number of registrations may receive special recognition or rewards.
  • Participants must ensure that their involvement does not affect regular academic activities or violate any institutional norms.
  • Ambassadors should inform or take consent from their respective college authorities/departments if required.
  • All promotional activities should be conducted in a professional and responsible manner, maintaining the reputation of both the college and the organizing committee.
